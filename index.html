What does the word 'Startup' mean in the context of the Indian Collegiate Startup Week 2022?

The effort put in by one founder, or more co-founders, to bring an innovative or new product or service to market for which there is a need is our interpretation of a Startup.

Let's clarify the key attributes of a Startup:

  • They can be either for-profit or non-profit. Many startups positively impact our planet and the environment and they are not geared solely towards churning profits for their founders.
  • They should either create something new or improve upon what already exists through innovation.
  • Though most startups are heavily reliant on technology, it need not always be the case,
  • Finally, a Startup is driven more by passion of its founders than any cold business logic.

What industry can I choose for my startup?

It is completely your choice (or the direction your idea drives you towards). Startups that have gone on to become unicorns in India have straddled diverse industries - SAAS, Healthcare, Personal Care, Real Estate, Food Delivery, Insurance, Cloud Kitchen, Jobs Search, Gaming, Cryptocurrency, Hotel Bookings, Travel Bookings, Technology, Logistics, E-commerce, Finance, IT, Marketing, Education and many more.

steps to register my team for Indian Collegiate Startup Week 2022?
Get along with your teams and make a remarkable startup plan
  • You need to first develop or have a general idea of the product or service you want to create or launch. This could be your idea or one proposed by your friend/s who intend to be part of your team.
  • Find out like-minded friends who are also college students like you who may want to join you in working on your project and have something to contribute towards the startup idea.
  • Take a photo of your college ID cards (all team members) and sit down to fill the registration form given below. One team member can complete details for all the other team members. The maximum number of members in a team can be 5 and the minimum 1.
  • Participation in Indian Collegiate Startup Week is completely free for all participants. No fee has to be paid at all.
